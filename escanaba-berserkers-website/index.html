About Us

Founded in 2024, the Escanaba Berserkers RFC is a grassroots rugby club based in Michigan’s Upper Peninsula. We train, compete, and grow the game of Rugby Union in our community. No pads, just pure rugby.

All skill levels welcome (16+)
No experience needed — learn on the pitch
Minimal gear required: cleats & mouthguard
Community-driven and player-run

How to Join

  1. Message us on WhatsApp/Text: (906) 261-8497
  2. Show up to practice: Wed/Thu 7PM at Ludington Park, Escanaba
  3. Bring cleats, water, and a mouthguard. We’ll supply the rest.
