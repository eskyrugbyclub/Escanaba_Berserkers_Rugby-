About the Club

Founded in 2024, Escanaba Berserkers RFC brings Rugby Union to Michigan’s U.P. We’re a grassroots, player‑run side competing in socials and 7s while we recruit toward 15s.

All skill levels welcome (16+ with guardian OK).
Minimal gear: cleats + mouthguard.
Learn-by-playing: safe contact & core skills.
Community‑first, volunteer‑led.
